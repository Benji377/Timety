# Privacy Policy for Timety

**Effective Date:** May 10, 2026

Benjamin Demetz ("Developer", "we", "us", or "our") built the Timety app as an Open Source, ad-free application. This SERVICE is provided by Benjamin Demetz at no cost and is intended for use as is.

This page is used to inform visitors regarding our policies with the collection, use, and disclosure of Personal Information if anyone decided to use our Service.

### 1. Data Collection and Use
**We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data.** Timety is designed to be a completely offline, privacy-first application. All data generated by your use of the app, including habit tracking, tasks, and time entries—is stored locally in the app's database directly on your device. We do not have servers, and your data never leaves your phone unless you manually export it yourself.

### 2. Third-Party Services and Analytics
This app does **not** use any third-party analytics, tracking libraries, or advertising frameworks. Your usage behavior is not monitored, recorded, or shared with any external services or companies.

### 3. Permissions
The app only requests the minimum device permissions necessary for its core functionality to operate. Because all data is handled locally, no internet permission is required for the app's primary features. 

### 4. Data Security
Since we do not collect or transmit your data, your information is protected by your device's own operating system security and encryption. We recommend securing your device with a PIN, password, or biometric lock to protect the local data stored by the app.

### 5. Children’s Privacy
These Services do not address anyone under the age of 13. Since we do not collect any personal data from any users, we inherently do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. 

### 6. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. These changes are effective immediately after they are posted.

### 7. Contact Us
If you have any questions or suggestions about this Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us.
